What is Doodle Jump Original?

Doodle Jump is a 2009 platforming video game developed and published by Lima Sky for iOS, Android, BlackBerry, Java ME, Windows Phone, Symbian OS, and bada. A port for the PlayStation 3 was also released in 2011. The objective of Doodle Jump is to guide an in-game character as high as possible on a series of platforms without falling off the screen. The game is noted for its simple gameplay, doodle-inspired graphics, and lack of verbal communication.
